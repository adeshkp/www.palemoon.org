Pale Moon Contact

We no longer offer direct contact with our developers due to required focus being drawn away from development to handle support requests despite the clear indicators of self-help resources and requests to use the normal channels to get technical support for Pale Moon. In addition, the increased amount of abuse initiated through web feedback forms would require a disproportionate amount of data verification going forward, which would benefit neither the developer nor you.

You are kindly requested to direct all your feedback to the community-supported Pale Moon forum:

https://forum.palemoon.org/

Please post in the public boards for any technical support questions or general feedback, taking care to use the board that best matches your type of question or feedback.
If you are an IRC user, you can also get in touch with some members of our community in chat by going to #palemoon on the Freenode network.

DO NOT SEND TECH SUPPORT QUESTIONS OR BUG RPORTS VIA E-MAIL!
They will not be responded to and will not be addressed or recorded.

Companies interested in contacting the Pale Moon developer directly are requested to register on the forum and initiate contact via Private Messages. If this is problematic, you may contact the developer via e-mail at business-inquiries@palemoon.org. Please understand that any non-business inquiries at that address will be discarded.
Important note for marketers: Please be aware that we no longer accept new applicants for corporate sponsorship with linkage, and we do not host promoted content or stories. If you are thinking of writing to us with a request for SEO/promotion on our website or blog/forum, please stop and save everyone involved valuable time and effort. Our project and product websites will not cater to your request. We may discard such requests without reply.

If you are a member of the press, please feel free to use press@palemoon.org as a point of contact.
If you want to get in touch for licensing our trademarks or legal matters, please use legal@palemoon.org as a point of contact.
