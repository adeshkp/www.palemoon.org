Pale Moon Feedback

The Pale Moon Feedback form is off-line until further notice, due to required focus being drawn away from development to handle support requests despite the clear indicators of self-help resources and requests to use the normal channels to get technical support for Pale Moon. In addition, the increased amount of abuse initiated through web feedback forms would require a disproportionate amount of data verification going forward, which would benefit neither the developer nor you.

You are kindly requested to direct all your feedback (including testimonials) to the community-supported Pale Moon forum:

http://forum.palemoon.org/

Please post in the public boards for any technical support questions or general feedback, taking care to use the board that best matches your type of question or feedback.

Companies interested in contacting the Pale Moon developer directly are requested to register on the forum and initiate contact via Private Messages. If this is problematic, you may contact the developer via e-mail at business-inquiries@palemoon.org. Please understand that any non-business inquiries at that address will be discarded.
