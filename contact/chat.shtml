Pale Moon Community Chat

Pale Moon has an IRC channel that is operated on the Freenode IRC network (a chat network mainly focusing on Free and Open Source Software). If you have an IRC-capable chat client then you can point it to the following address:

irc://irc.freenode.net:6667/#palemoon

Alternatively, you can enter our IRC channel directly from the browser and have a chat!
Please be patient if you have a question, and keep in mind that this is a public IRC chat room, not private support chat like you may be used to from chatting with commercial companies. If you don't get an answer, it's recommended that you post on the forum instead. People on IRC commonly idle in chat rooms and may not actually see your questions or posts until a lot later - although the chat is real-time, and you may get an instant dialogue going, it may also not be immediate.

Please follow expected etiquette when joining this chat room. Thanks in advance! :)
