Pale Moon Live Chat etiquette

Pale Moon has an IRC channel that is operated on the Freenode IRC network (a chat network mainly focusing on Free and Open Source Software). While on the chat network and in the chat room, you are requested to adhere to the following etiquette rules. Not following these rules can get you removed from the chatroom (temporarily or permanently, depending on behavior):

  • The chatroom is meant for users of an adult mindset. Please only use the chatroom if you are of adult age or have the mental capacity equivalent of an adult.
  • Please mind your language. The chatroom is a public room, and offending people with excessive use of coarse language is frowned upon. Although everyone is assumed to be of adult age (see previous point), and you may be upset about something, you're kindly requested to keep your use of language under control.
  • Please keep in mind that the chatroom will be used by people from all walks of life, and all levels of computer skill. It is meant to be a place to offer help, and if you cannot do so, or a problem is too complex, or you find that it is too difficult to translate a solution into something that can be easily understood in chat and/or by the person asking the question, then please forward them in a kind manner to the Pale Moon forum. Talking down on people because they are not "power users" will be grounds for immediate removal from the channel.
  • Please do not use ALL CAPS when chatting. Please try to use understandable spelling and please use punctuation. Making yourself clearly understood is in everyone's interest :)
  • Please use English at all times. Languages other than English should be taken to private chat or other channels specifically catering to the non-English language spoken.
  • Please do not try to unnecessarily mask your identity. We are all people there, with personalities and identities. It is much easier to relate to and assist someone that actually tries to approach others with a personal identity than someone who goes at length to mask themselves. None of the topics in the chatroom will be personally or ideologically sensitive if we can help it, so there will be no need to hide/cloak/mask yourself.
  • Please do not start flame wars or personal attacks against other users visiting the channel.
  • Please do not troll or be disruptive to the chat.
  • Please do not start discussions about politics, religion, or other touchy subjects that are not necessarily related to the browser (off-topic) and can easily lead to an very unwelcome atmosphere. Although enforcing of this point will be done leniently, you are asked to apply common sense and stop a particular line of conversation if it gets too heated or opinionated for the channel's overall atmosphere. If you want to continue an off-topic or in-depth discussion with someone, please take it to private chat!
