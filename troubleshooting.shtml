Pale Moon: Troubleshooting

Problems?

Of course, a complex piece of software like this web browser is not without its bugs or problems.

A few self-help resources:

The Pale Moon forum: http://forum.palemoon.org/
Keep in mind that the forum is not a read-only resource! If you can't find the answer to your question, you are encouraged to make a new topic thread and talk to the Pale Moon community and fellow users in it to solve your issues.

The Mozillazine knowledgebase, which can be found here: http://kb.mozillazine.org/Mozilla_Firefox
This KB is useful for general usage questions and support information about features shared with Mozilla Firefox.
