Pale Moon Cookie Policy

Pale Moon related websites use cookies to store some required information and settings for proper operation. These cookies do not contain any sensitive information and are used for:
  1. Proper operation of the website. For example, your session information when visiting the forum or logging in to the forum or start portal are stored in a cookie. This functionality will break when cookies are disallowed.
  2. Advertising. We use advertising on some of our websites to offset the costs involved in providing the website and services related to Pale Moon to you. The advertising networks generally use cookies as part of their advertising framework, to keep track of the validity of visits and clicks. By displaying advertisements through this framework, relevant cookies will be stored in your browser.
  3. Content delivery. Our content delivery network may store some (anonymous) cookies in your browser when you visit our sites.
By using these websites, you consent to the use of cookies.

This policy and information is required by EU laws on the protection of privacy of Internet users.

If you do not consent to the use of cookies and still want to use the websites:
  1. All modern browsers offer user choice to allow the storage and use of cookies, either temporarily or permanently. How exactly these browser settings are made depends on the browser in use; please see the help file or manual for your software to obtain specific information on how to make these browser settings.
  2. Some browser extensions may help in blocking third-party cookies like those used by the advertisements on our websites, which may provide you with finer granularity as to what you choose to allow.
Please note that disallowing cookies may impact your user experience and the usability of our websites. For technical reasons, we cannot offer support to visitors who run into issues when cookies are not allowed or selectively blocked.

If you have any questions about this policy, or think anything is in error, then please contact me.
