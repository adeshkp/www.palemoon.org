Pale Moon Donations and Support

If you enjoy this free browser enough to want to give something in return, there are several ways you can support development of Pale Moon. If you want your donation to remain anonymous, please make sure to let me know!

Monetary donations

You can transfer money by using one of the following methods:
  • Paypal - this is a well-known US-based electronic money provider. You can use the donation buttons on the Pale Moon home page or Pale Moon start page to make a donation through PayPal.
  • Skrill (formerly Moneybookers) - this is a European-based electronic money provider similar to PayPal, with lower fees and better monetary regulation, accepted world-wide with many options for payment/funding (no credit card required to use it).
    Simply transfer money as a personal transfer (yellow "send money" button) to
    Name: M.C. Straver
    E-mail: moonchild [at] palemoon.org
    Don't have an account? Sign up here.
  • Bank (wire) transfer
    For people living in the EU, this is an option as well (sorry, I don't accept wire transfers from outside the EU zone due to fees) as long as your bank offers SEPA transfers. Contact me to get the bank details.
  • Bitcoin: You can donate any amount you wish to Moonchild Productions' wallet:
    1PaLEmooNYSouqxNZU2U4zHaSRfPBH6ifE
    Please note that Bitcoin is anonymous by nature and you will not be listed by name on this page.
  • Litecoin: A different, popular cryptocurrency. Feel free to donate any amount to:
    LPRK7FtFKNqrVTHhynHbZw4xJA7QL4w7go
    Please note that Litecoin is anonymous by nature and you will not be listed by name on this page.
  • Other cryptocurrencies are accepted too! See http://forum.palemoon.org/viewtopic.php?f=24&t=4044
  • Sending cash (bills only please, no coins)
    If you prefer, you can also send a donation in the form of cash (any currency accepted, but Swedish Crowns preferred. Sending money in an envelope is always at your own risk; please make sure to not make it obvious as it may get stolen by postal workers in that case.

Donating development hardware

If you wish to donate (surplus) hardware to help development of Pale Moon, you should contact me by e-mail at moonchild [at] palemoon.org to discuss if what you wish to donate will be useful for me (saves unnecessary shipping expenses and saves stuff taking up my floor space ;) ).

Currently, I could use, specifically:
  • A compact, low-power PC with 2 network interfaces for server networking
  • A new large-capacity (4 TB or larger) SATA-drive for backup purposes or a large-capacity (>500GB) tape drive+media
  • A development class OSX 10.8+ Macintosh computer (to be able to build Pale Moon for Mac)

Donating services

If you want to supply services to help pale Moon development, there are a few possible areas you may be able to help. Please contact me to discuss this.

Potential service areas:
  • Advertising! Spread the word, launch an ad campaign, tell your friends, hand out flyers, etc.
  • File hosting (burst capable, direct-link capable, zero-ads) (any location except DE)
  • Cloud computing services/build machines
  • Any other service you think may be of benefit to Pale Moon development
