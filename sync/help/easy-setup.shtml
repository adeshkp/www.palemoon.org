Pale Moon Sync service

How do I Add a New Device to my Sync account?

Perform these steps in Pale Moon on a computer that already has your Sync account set up.

  1. Click the Pale Moon button (or go to the Tools menu) and click Options.
  2. In the Options window, go to Sync, then click the Pair a device link and the Sync Setup window will open.

  3. Get your new device, and do the following:
    1. If your other device is another computer (desktop or laptop):
      Go to "Set Up Sync..." in the program's menu, then click "I already have an account".
      Sync setup will now give you a 12-character code.
    2. If your other device is a mobil (Android phone or tablet):
      Tap the Set up Pale Moon Sync button on the Start page. (Note: You can also access Sync through the main menu More > Settings > Sync)
      Sync setup will now give you a 12-character code.
  4. Enter this twelve character code generated on your new device and click Next.
  5. When the code is accepted you will see a Device Connected message and your device will immediately begin syncing. Click Finish to close the setup window.

How do I Add a Device when I'm not near my computer?

Perform these steps on the device you are trying to add to Pale Moon Sync.

If you are trying to add a device to Pale Moon Sync and you are not near your computer so that you can enter the code, you can still get it done by entering your account information.

Add an Android device

Perform these steps in Pale Moon on your Android device.

  1. Tap the Set up Pale Moon Sync button on the Start page.
    Note: You can also access Sync through the main menu More > Settings > Sync
  2. Tap the "Advanced setup..." link.
  3. Next, enter your Account Name (email address), Password, Recovery Key and tap Connect.

Add another computer

Perform these steps on the computer you are trying to add to Pale Moon Sync.

  1. At the top of the Pale Moon window, click the Pale Moon button and select Set Up Sync.
    The Pale Moon Sync Setup window will open.
  2. Click I Have an Account.
  3. Since you're not near the device with Sync already set up, click the I don't have the device with me link.
  4. Sign in to your Sync account:
    • Enter your account email address
    • Enter your password
    • Enter your Recovery Key
    • Then click Next
  5. You will see a Setup Complete screen. Click Finish to complete adding this device to your Sync account.
